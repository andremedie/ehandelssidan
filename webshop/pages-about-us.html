About Us

So it all began 2017. E-commerce is growing in lightning speed, and we decided to create a good webshop for clothing that you can use 24/7 - in all conditions, between 4 seasons. We loved the idea that shopping should be easy, at only one place (Emotions.com, of course) and that every stitch is made with great skill and thoughtfulness.

We are a team of 25 hungry students with different views about how this company should be driven. Some of us in the team say we do it for fun, some say lets go all-in and some say this idea is crazy but necessary. So we create the clothing in different parts of Europe, and we sell them all over the world. Italy makes great jeans, Germany makes great shirts, and Sweden makes great Boots and Hats - together it becomes clear that Earth is supposed to work together to make great business and caring for the people around the world.
